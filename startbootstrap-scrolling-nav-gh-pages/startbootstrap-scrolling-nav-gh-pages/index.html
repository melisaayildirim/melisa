About Our University

Founded in 2018, Sivas Science and Technology University is a state university specializing in engineering, agriculture, and aerospace, focusing on applied research.

Mission

To contribute to our country and humanity through science, technology, and innovation-based education.

Vision

To be internationally recognized as a leading university in research and technology production.

Values

Scientific integrity, innovation, community service, and sustainability.

Our Faculties

Faculty of Engineering and Natural Sciences

Offers technology-oriented education with departments such as Mechanical, Software, Electrical-Electronics, and Industrial Engineering.

Faculty of Agriculture Sciences and Technologies

Carries out applied studies in agriculture, food technologies, and biosystems engineering.

Faculty of Aviation and Space Sciences

Provides research and education in aviation management and aircraft engineering.

Research and Projects

Our university strengthens R&D activities through industry collaboration, innovative projects, and national funding support.

Defense Technologies

Innovative projects contributing to the national defense industry are carried out.

Artificial Intelligence Applications

Research on autonomous systems, image processing, and data analytics is conducted.

Digital Transformation in Agriculture

Smart agriculture technologies enhance production efficiency.
